# SWE 1:1 Notes

Use this skill only in the My Work workspace for software engineering manager 1:1 notes. Do not use it for source-code repositories, implementation tasks, product specs, or general engineering analysis.

## Instructions

1. Treat the input as private manager notes.
2. Preserve facts separately from interpretations.
3. Extract concise themes, follow-ups, commitments, risks, coaching signals, and open questions.
4. Keep the output practical and manager-ready.
5. Avoid inventing details that are not present in the notes.

## Output Format

### Summary

Briefly summarize the conversation.

### Follow-ups

List concrete follow-ups with owners when known.

### Commitments

List commitments made by either person.

### Risks or Concerns

Call out schedule, morale, collaboration, execution, or ambiguity risks.

### Coaching or Career Signals

Capture growth areas, strengths, aspirations, or feedback evidence.

### Next 1:1 Prep

List topics to revisit next time.
